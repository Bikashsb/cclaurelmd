Pet Odor Removal That Actually Works in Laurel, MD

Standard carpet cleaning removes surface stains but rarely eliminates pet urine odor — because urine crystals in the carpet backing and subfloor are never reached. Carpet Cleaning Laurel MD uses professional enzyme treatment that breaks down urine at the molecular level, eliminating the odor source rather than masking it.

This is why pet odors in Laurel homes often return in July — and why professional enzyme treatment is more effective than deodorizer spray.

Our Pet Odor Removal Process

1

UV Light Inspection

We use UV black lights to identify all urine deposit locations, including old stains invisible to the naked eye. Some Laurel homes have previous pet damage from prior tenants that was never fully treated.

2

Enzyme Pre-Treatment

Professional enzyme solution is saturated into the carpet backing and padding — not just the surface. The enzyme dwells for the required time to digest uric acid crystals fully.

3

Hot Water Extraction

220°F hot water extraction flushes treated waste from carpet fiber and backing. The high temperature also sanitizes. Any remaining surface stain is treated with color-safe spotters.
